History

The MUN Grand Prix represents a revolutionary approach to Model United Nations conferences, combining traditional diplomatic simulation with competitive elements inspired by grand prix racing. Since its inception, this unique format has challenged delegates to navigate complex international issues while racing against time and competing teams.


What started as an experimental format has grown into one of the most anticipated MUN events, attracting delegates from around the globe who seek both intellectual challenge and competitive excitement.
